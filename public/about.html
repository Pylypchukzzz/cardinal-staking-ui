ABOUT BIOPUNK STAKING

Biopunk staking is a free way for holders of our NFTs to get the $BIO gaming token. Which serves as a full-fledged currency for payment within our Web 3.0 ecosystem. All transactions will take place only in this token.
Our goal is to let our investors get our BIO token for free. Thanks to a voluntary NFT hold for a certain period of time (minimum 14 days). This will allow you to earn money by selling tokens or have capital to start and improve characters in the game itself.
The NFT hold is completely voluntary and untracked.
